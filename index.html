About

The need to constantly learn new ways to create interesting, memorable, and sharable experiences whether that be through technology, art, or music is really what has led me down the path of becoming a full stack web developer. The passion and drive for excellence, I have when creating music transferred over almost seamlessly into the way I approach development on a new project. I would say I have a huge passion for the UI/UX design side of a project, but I really enjoy being able to jump in on any part of the development whether that be frontend or backend code and learning how everything is working together.

When coding in my free time I enjoy creating projects inspired by my other passions and seeing how I can solve real life problems that I or people I work with struggle with in their daily creative space. Outside of web development some of my hobbies include producing music, digital painting, brushing up on my photoshop skills, and also taking care of all my plant friends. Pursuing Web develoment has been such an eye opening experience for me so far and I really look forward to being exposed to new tech and developing my skills and creating more experiences I am proud of.

Bit Synth

Bit Synth is an in browser polyphonic synthesizer and step sequencer built with the help of the tone.js library. It allows the user to shape soundwaves, create and save synth presets to contribute to a public library of synth presets inspired by retro 8 bit and 16 bit video games
